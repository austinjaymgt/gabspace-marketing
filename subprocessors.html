Subprocessors

The third-party service providers Gabspace uses to operate the platform. Each is bound by contract to protect your information consistent with our Privacy Policy.

Last updated: May 14, 2026

Gabspace, a product of Austin Jay Management LLC, uses the following third-party service providers ("subprocessors") to operate the Service. We update this page when subprocessors change. To be notified of changes, email privacy@gabspace.io and ask to be added to our subprocessor notification list.

Current subprocessors

Subprocessor Purpose Data processed Location
Supabase, Inc.supabase.com Database, authentication, file storage, edge functions Account information, workspace content, uploaded files, authentication tokens United States
Vercel Inc.vercel.com Application hosting and content delivery network All data transmitted to and from the Service, IP addresses, request logs US + Global edge
Resendresend.com Transactional and marketing email delivery Recipient email addresses, names, email content United States
Anthropic, PBCanthropic.com AI-powered features (brainstorm and concept generation). Per Anthropic's commercial API terms, customer data is not used to train Anthropic's models. Content you submit to AI features United States
Zapier, Inc.zapier.com Internal workflow notifications for the Gabspace team Limited account event data (new accounts, key activity) United States

Hosting and storage location

Gabspace primarily stores and processes data in the United States. Some data may transit through global edge networks (e.g., Vercel's CDN) for performance reasons.

For users in the EU, UK, or Switzerland: transfers of personal information to the United States are made under Standard Contractual Clauses approved by the European Commission (and the UK International Data Transfer Addendum where applicable), as described in our Privacy Policy.

Changes to this list

When we add a new subprocessor, we'll update this page and (where required by law or by our customer contracts) give advance notice. If you have any questions or concerns about a new subprocessor, email privacy@gabspace.io.
