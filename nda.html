This Beta Tester Non-Disclosure Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into between Austin Jay Management LLC, a Georgia limited liability company doing business as Gabspace ("Company"), and the individual who accepts this Agreement electronically ("Beta Tester").

By checking the acknowledgment box in the Gabspace application, Beta Tester agrees to be bound by the terms of this Agreement as of the date of acceptance ("Effective Date").

1. Purpose

Company is developing Gabspace, a business management platform for creative entrepreneurs (the "Product"). Beta Tester has been invited to access a pre-release version of the Product for the purpose of testing and providing feedback ("Beta Program"). In connection with participation in the Beta Program, Beta Tester may receive or be exposed to Confidential Information as defined below.

2. Confidential Information

"Confidential Information" means any non-public information disclosed by Company to Beta Tester in connection with the Beta Program, including but not limited to:

  • The Product's features, functionality, design, and roadmap
  • Unreleased screenshots, videos, or demonstrations of the Product
  • Pricing, business strategy, and go-to-market plans
  • Technical architecture, code, or infrastructure details
  • Any other information designated as confidential or that a reasonable person would understand to be confidential given the nature of the disclosure

Confidential Information does not include information that: (a) is or becomes publicly known through no breach of this Agreement; (b) was already known to Beta Tester prior to disclosure; (c) is independently developed by Beta Tester without reference to Confidential Information; or (d) is required to be disclosed by law or court order, provided Beta Tester gives Company reasonable prior notice.

3. Beta Tester Obligations

Beta Tester agrees to:

  1. Keep all Confidential Information strictly confidential and not disclose it to any third party
  2. Use Confidential Information solely for the purpose of participating in the Beta Program
  3. Not reproduce, publish, or distribute screenshots, recordings, or descriptions of the Product without prior written consent from Company
  4. Notify Company promptly upon becoming aware of any unauthorized disclosure or use of Confidential Information
  5. Return or destroy Confidential Information upon Company's written request or upon termination of this Agreement

4. Feedback

Beta Tester may voluntarily provide suggestions, ideas, bug reports, or other feedback regarding the Product ("Feedback"). Beta Tester agrees that Company may freely use, disclose, reproduce, license, distribute, and otherwise exploit any Feedback without restriction and without any obligation, royalty, or additional consideration to Beta Tester. Beta Tester hereby assigns to Company all rights in and to such Feedback.

5. No License or Ownership Rights

Nothing in this Agreement grants Beta Tester any ownership interest in or license to the Product, Company's intellectual property, or any Confidential Information beyond the limited right to use the Product during the Beta Program solely for evaluation purposes.

6. No Reverse Engineering

Beta Tester agrees not to reverse engineer, decompile, disassemble, or otherwise attempt to derive the source code or underlying structure of the Product or any component thereof.
