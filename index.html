Built around how creatives actually work

Not a generic CRM. Not a complicated suite. Gabspace is structured for the way creative businesses move — from first inquiry to final delivery.

Client management
Track every client from lead to completed project with a built-in sales funnel — Lead, Prospect, Active, Completed.
Project profiles
Budget items, file uploads, contingency tracking, and project status — all in one shareable project view.
Simple finances
Track revenue and expenses without the complexity. Monthly snapshots, YTD totals, and receipt uploads included.
Client portal
Share project updates with clients in a clean portal. They can react and comment — no login required.
Marketing tracker
Log campaigns and events, set goals, track outcomes, and see what's actually driving new business.
Business dashboard
A personalized overview of your business health — KPIs, recent activity, and upcoming work at a glance.
